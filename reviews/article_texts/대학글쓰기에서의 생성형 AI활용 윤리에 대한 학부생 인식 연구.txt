2. 생성형 AI와 글쓰기 교육
AI﻿기술은﻿글쓰기﻿분야에서﻿작문﻿자동﻿채점과﻿이독성﻿지수﻿자동﻿측정﻿등에﻿
활용되어﻿왔다.﻿이는﻿AI를﻿인간이﻿산출한﻿텍스트를﻿평가하거나﻿텍스트의﻿특
성을﻿분석하는﻿차원에서﻿활용한﻿것이었다.﻿하지만﻿ChatGPT로
﻿대표되는﻿생
성형﻿AI의﻿보급과﻿사용은﻿인간이﻿아닌﻿AI가﻿텍스트를﻿산출하는﻿것이기에﻿이
는﻿글쓰기﻿분야에﻿큰﻿변화를﻿불러일으킨﻿것이다.
생성형﻿AI는﻿인공지능의﻿한﻿종류로,﻿AI가﻿
“이미지,﻿비디오,﻿오디오,﻿텍스트﻿
등을﻿포함한﻿대량의﻿데이터를﻿학습하여﻿사람과﻿유사한﻿방식으로﻿문맥과﻿의미
를﻿이해하고﻿새로운﻿데이터를﻿대량으로﻿생성해주는﻿기술”을﻿일컫는다(국가정보
원,﻿2023:1).﻿대표적인﻿생성형﻿AI﻿모델에는﻿ChatGPT,﻿Bard,﻿DALL-E﻿2,﻿Stable﻿
Diffusion﻿등이﻿있다.﻿ChatGPT는﻿OpenAI에서﻿개발한﻿대규모﻿언어﻿모델로﻿
텍스트,﻿이미지,﻿음성을﻿입력하면﻿ChatGPT는﻿입력을﻿분석한﻿후﻿적절한﻿응답
을﻿생성하여﻿사용자에게﻿제시한다.﻿그리고﻿Bard는﻿Google에서﻿개발한﻿대규
모
﻿언어﻿모델이며﻿ChatGPT와﻿같이﻿사용자의﻿질문에﻿텍스트나﻿이미지﻿등을﻿
사용해﻿적절한﻿응답을﻿제공한다.﻿또한﻿DALL-E﻿2와﻿Stable﻿Diffusion는﻿이
미지를﻿생성하는﻿AI﻿모델로﻿DALL-E﻿2는﻿OpenAI에서﻿개발하였으며﻿Stable﻿
Diffusion은﻿Stability﻿AI가﻿개발하였다.﻿두﻿AI는﻿입력된﻿텍스트﻿정보를﻿활용
해﻿이미지를﻿생성하여﻿사용자에게﻿제공하는﻿생성형﻿AI﻿모델이다.

Undergraduates’ Awareness of the Ethics of Generative AI
Utilization in College Writing15)
Oh Sunkyung (Korea University)
Jang Mijung (Korea University)
Park Jung-eun (Korea University)
This study examines the utilization of generative AI among undergraduate stu-
dents and their awareness of ethics in college writing. It also identifies the demand
for ethics education related to writing using generative AI. A survey was conducted
on 895 undergraduate students taking college writing courses at A University.
The study found that first, undergraduate students who have had experience with
university writing tasks employed generative AI used ChatGPT the most; they used
it substantially to generate ideas and collect data when writing general reports. In
addition, more than half of the experienced users were satisfied with generative AI.
Undergraduate students comply with writing ethics when performing college writ-
ing and are well aware of the problems in using generative AI, but they have low
awareness of citation methods in writing when using generative AI. There was a
high educational demand for citation methods and writing ethics when performing
writing using generative AI. Moreover, there were some differences in the level of
awareness and educational needs depending on the undergraduate major and the
experience of using generative AI.
Accordingly, this study suggested the following measures in college writing sub-
jects: education on how to comply with writing ethics and citation when using gen-
erative AI for writing, education that reflects the differences in students’ awareness
